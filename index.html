About Me

Hi! I’m Joseph Chen, a Robotics Master's student at Johns Hopkins University with a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering from Purdue University.

I am a product-focused mechanical engineer passionate about robotics, medtech innovation, and turning ideas into functional designs. I have hands-on experience in CAD/3D modeling, FEA, rapid prototyping, manufacturing, computer vision/Deep learning, mechatronics, and robotics.

I enjoy tackling a broad range of engineering challenges and bridging the gap between hardware and software to create impactful solutions.

Outside of engineering, I find inspiration in the outdoors — whether it’s climbing, sketching, or exploring new places.
