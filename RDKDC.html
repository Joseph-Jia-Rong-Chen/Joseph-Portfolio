Virtuoso Robot Operating Systems

Johns Hopkins University

Download Report

The RDKDC project focused on developing and validating advanced control strategies for a UR5 robotic arm to achieve high-precision manipulation tasks. We implemented and compared three control algorithms—Inverse Kinematics, Resolved Rate, and Jacobian-Transpose—achieving sub-centimeter accuracy in both simulation and hardware. The system integrated forward kinematics modeling, collision detection, singularity avoidance, and real-time safety monitoring through determinant-based Jacobian analysis. Additionally, a computer vision pipeline was engineered to enable autonomous image reproduction, leveraging binary image processing, morphological skeleton extraction, and Traveling Salesman Algorithm optimization for efficient path planning.
